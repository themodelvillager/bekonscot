                                    Splashyng

                                    The noise of the morris men in the square is only drowned out by the rumble of approaching trains at regular intervals. Goodness only knows how the WI meeting inside the Town Hall manages to keep going with that racket. Take a look inside and you’ll see the formidable Chairwoman lecturing the group!

                                    Look out too for:

                                    • Aerodrome with its planes taking off
                                    • Clifftop tramway
                                    • Beaches, Wychwood Castle and whales in the lake
                                    • Moving Yacht Race
                                    • Alexandra Bridge (based on Sydney Harbour Bridge)
                                    • Working level crossing
                                    • Tudor House with transparent walls
                                    • Elevated Walkway
